Business & Corporate Taxes.

Entity returns and compliance for C-Corps, S-Corps, partnerships, multi-member LLCs, trusts, and estates — with entity-selection and compensation guidance throughout the year.

What's Included

The full scope of work.

Each engagement begins with a written scope and a flat or hourly fee disclosed in advance, in compliance with Circular 230 §10.27.

C-Corporation (Form 1120)

Double-taxation entities — federal and MA Form 355.

  • Federal Form 1120
  • MA Form 355
  • Accumulated earnings analysis
  • Book-tax M-1/M-3 reconciliation

S-Corporation (Form 1120-S)

Pass-through entities with reasonable-compensation planning built into the engagement.

  • Federal Form 1120-S
  • K-1 generation for shareholders
  • Reasonable compensation analysis
  • MA Form 355S

Partnerships / LLC (Form 1065)

Multi-member LLCs, partnerships, and joint ventures with K-1 allocations and basis tracking.

  • Federal Form 1065
  • K-1s with special allocations
  • §754 elections
  • Guaranteed payments

Trusts & Estates (Form 1041)

Fiduciary income tax returns, grantor letters, and beneficiary K-1s.

  • Federal Form 1041
  • Grantor trust letters
  • Distributable net income (DNI)
  • MA Form 2

Entity Selection & Review

Choosing — or re-evaluating — the right legal and tax structure for your business.

  • LLC vs. S-Corp vs. C-Corp analysis
  • §199A QBI planning
  • State tax impact modeling
  • Conversion mechanics

Depreciation & Capex

§179 expensing, bonus depreciation, and accountable-plan setup to move money tax-efficiently.

  • §179 and bonus depreciation
  • Accountable plan policies
  • Multi-state apportionment
  • Cost segregation coordination
Our Process

Four steps, end to end.

The same workflow whether the engagement is a single 1040 or a multi-entity consolidation — predictable, documented, and auditable.

01

Scoping

Entity structure, books quality, and prior-year return reviewed; engagement scope confirmed in writing.

02

Books Review

Trial balance reconciled to bank statements; any adjustments proposed and approved before filing.

03

Return Prep

Federal and state returns prepared with full workpapers, M-1/M-3 reconciliation, and K-1s (if applicable).

04

Filing & Planning

E-file federal and MA, deliver K-1s, and schedule the Q1 estimated-tax planning call for the next year.

Transparent flat-fee or hourly pricing.
