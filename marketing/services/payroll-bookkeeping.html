Payroll & Bookkeeping.

Ongoing bookkeeping, payroll processing, and employment tax filings for Massachusetts businesses — with QuickBooks ProAdvisor setup and monthly close.

What's Included

The full scope of work.

Each engagement begins with a written scope and a flat or hourly fee disclosed in advance, in compliance with Circular 230 §10.27.

Payroll Processing

Weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly payroll runs with direct deposit and employee pay stubs.

  • Direct deposit setup
  • Employee pay stubs
  • PTO accrual tracking
  • Multi-state payroll

Employment Tax Filings

Federal and state employment tax deposits and quarterly/annual returns.

  • Form 941 quarterly federal
  • Form 940 annual FUTA
  • MA UI wage reports
  • MA withholding filings

W-2 & 1099 Preparation

Year-end information returns for employees and independent contractors.

  • W-2 / W-3 preparation
  • 1099-NEC for contractors
  • 1099-MISC for rents
  • 1099-INT and 1099-DIV

Monthly Bookkeeping

QuickBooks Online monthly close — categorization, reconciliation, and financial statements.

  • Transaction categorization
  • Bank and credit card reconciliation
  • Monthly P&L and balance sheet
  • Cash flow statement

QuickBooks ProAdvisor

New-system setup, chart-of-accounts design, and cleanup of existing QuickBooks files.

Custom Reports

Our Process

Four steps, end to end.

The same workflow whether the engagement is a single 1040 or a multi-entity consolidation — predictable, documented, and auditable.

01

Onboarding

QBO subscription, chart of accounts, bank feeds, and payroll setup completed in week one.

02

Monthly Close

Transactions categorized, accounts reconciled, and draft financials prepared by the 10th of the following month.

03

Payroll Cycle

Payroll processed on schedule, tax deposits made timely, and pay stubs delivered to employees.

04

Quarterly Review

Financials reviewed with you each quarter; employment tax returns (941, MA UI) filed on deadline.

Transparent flat-fee or hourly pricing.
