IRS Representation

When the IRS has questions, you need answers — and someone between you and them.

An Enrolled Agent has unlimited rights to represent taxpayers before the Internal Revenue Service in all 50 states. That means audits, appeals, collections, and complex notices — we can handle them all.

Service Fee What's included
IRS Representation — Hourly $200/hour Billed in 15-minute increments. Covers anything not listed as a flat-fee package: appeals, collection due process hearings, Tax Court preparation, complex audits, unfiled return reviews.
IRS Matching Notice Response (CP2000 Underreporter Notice) $750 flat You received a notice saying the IRS's records don't match your return. We review the notice, prepare a written response, and submit it — all for one tax year. Appeals or multiple years are quoted separately.
Audit Defense — By Mail (Correspondence Audit, one year) $1,200 flat An IRS audit conducted entirely by mail for one tax year, no business income. We prepare every response, assemble your documents, and handle all IRS contact. Appeals are separate.
Audit Defense — In Person (Office or Field Audit, one year) $2,500 flat An in-person IRS audit for one tax year on an individual return. We go to the meeting. We bring the documents. You don't have to face the IRS alone. Appeals are separate.
Audit Defense — Business or Multi-Year $3,500 starting Business income, multiple years, or a payroll tax audit. We quote the estimated hours up front and the $200/hour rate applies after the $3,500 retainer is used. Scope confirmed in writing.
Penalty Abatement — First-Time $500 flat If the IRS has hit you with a failure-to-file or failure-to-pay penalty and you qualify for first-time relief, we prepare and submit the request for one tax year.
Penalty Abatement — Reasonable Cause $750 flat You had a legitimate reason for missing a deadline. We build the written narrative and submit supporting documentation to the IRS — one year, one penalty type.
Installment Agreement Setup $750 flat You owe the IRS and need a payment plan. We negotiate and set up the agreement, including any required financial statements. Partial-payment plans and complex cases bill hourly.
Currently Not Collectible Status (CNC Hardship) $850 flat If you genuinely can't pay, we prepare the hardship application, the financial statement, and the IRS submission that pauses collections.
Offer in Compromise — Starting (Form 656) $3,500 starting Settle your tax debt for less than you owe, when you qualify. Most cases land between $3,500 and $6,000. Includes the financial analysis, the offer calculation, and full IRS negotiation. The $205 IRS application fee is paid by you separately.
Back Taxes — Prior-Year Returns $350 / year (individual) • $750 / year (business) Unfiled returns brought current so you can move on. Base pricing is for simple returns — more complex prior years follow the standard schedule above.

Our fee commitment

Reasonable fees. Written scope. Records returned on request.

Transparent fees. Defensible work. Real relationships.

  • Reasonable, flat or hourly fees — never contingent fees. We don't charge a percentage of your refund or tie our fee to any tax outcome on your return.
